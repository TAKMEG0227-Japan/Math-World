# 🧮 Standard Problem 2 

"Try to solve it using only elementary school knowledge!"

There was a line of 800 people at the entrance of a stadium.
Additionally, 20 people join the line every minute.

When 5 gates were opened, the entire line cleared in 10 minutes.

Question:
How many gates would have been needed to clear the line in 5 minutes?
